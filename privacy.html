Data We Collect

This website does not use cookies, tracking tools, or analytics services. No personal data is actively collected or stored by us.

When you visit this website, your browser automatically transmits technical data to our hosting provider (e.g. IP address, browser type, date and time of access). This data is processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a secure and functional website) and is not used to identify individual users.

Fonts

This website uses self-hosted web fonts. No font files are loaded from external servers (e.g. Google Fonts). Your IP address is not transmitted to third parties in connection with font loading.

External Links

This website contains links to external services (e.g. SoundCloud, Instagram, LinkedIn). When you follow these links, the respective provider's privacy policy applies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for external content.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at ola@flui.space.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority (CNPD — Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados, cnpd.pt).
